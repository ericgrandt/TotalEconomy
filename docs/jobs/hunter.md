# Hunter

As a Hunter, you'll be rewarded for hunting animals.

## Default Rewards

| Material     | Money | Experience |
|--------------|-------|------------|
| chicken      | 2.50  | 10         |
| rabbit       | 2.50  | 10         |
| cow          | 2.50  | 10         |
| pig          | 2.50  | 10         |
| sheep        | 2.50  | 10         |
